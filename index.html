About Us

About Srishtika Learning Centre

Founded in 2015, Srishtika Learning Centre has been at the forefront of specialized education and therapy for children with diverse learning needs. Our centre was established with the vision of creating an inclusive, supportive environment where every child can thrive regardless of their learning differences.

At Srishtika, we believe in a holistic approach to child development, combining evidence-based therapeutic techniques with creative learning methodologies. Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to unlocking each child's potential and helping them develop the skills needed for academic success and personal growth.

What sets us apart:

  • Individualized attention and personalized learning plans
  • Integrated multi-disciplinary approach
  • Regular progress assessments and parent consultations
  • Warm, welcoming environment designed for optimal learning

Early Intervention Specialists

Children Cognitive Skill Development

Programs to enhance memory, attention, problem-solving, and other critical thinking skills.

Intellectual Disability Instruction

Remedial Education

Cookery Classes for Baking

Fun, interactive baking sessions that develop fine motor skills, following instructions, and creativity.

Job Orientation Training

Handwriting Classes

Drawing Classes
